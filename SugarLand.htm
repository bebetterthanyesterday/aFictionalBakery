City of Sugar Land

 

Sugar Land is a city in Texas within the Fort Bend County. It is one of the most affluent and fastest growing cities in Texas. Founded as a sugar plantation in the early mid-20th century and incorporated in 1959, Sugar Land is the largest city and economic center of Fort Bend County. It has the most master-planned communities in Fort Bend County, which is home to the largest number of master-planned communities in the nation—including First Colony, Sugar Creek, Riverstone, New Territory, Telfair, and many others. Sugar Land will soon be the home of a minor-league baseball team and a new stadium
